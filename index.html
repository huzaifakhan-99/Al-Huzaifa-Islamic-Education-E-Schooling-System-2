Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need for online classes?

A smartphone, tablet or computer with a stable internet connection. Classes are held over a simple video call — no special software required.

Is the trial class really completely free?

Yes — 100% free, no credit card, no obligation. Every new student gets one full trial class with their matched teacher before any payment is made.

Can my child combine Qur'an classes with the School Program?

Absolutely. Many of our students do both. We build a combined timetable so Qur'an and school classes never clash and the child gets the best of both.

How are teachers verified?

Every Qur'an teacher is tested on Tajweed and must show a valid ijazah. School teachers are verified for qualifications and experience. We also do reference checks before anyone joins our team.

What if the teacher is not a good fit for my child?

No problem at all. We'll rematch your child with a different teacher at no extra cost until you're completely satisfied with the pairing.

How does payment work?
